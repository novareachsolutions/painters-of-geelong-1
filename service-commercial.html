FAQ

Got Questions? We've
Got Answers!

Can you paint outside of business hours?

Absolutely. We regularly work evenings, weekends, and overnight to minimise disruption to your business operations. We'll schedule around your needs.

Do you handle large commercial projects?

Yes, we have the team and equipment to handle projects of all sizes — from small offices to large warehouses and multi-storey buildings.

What types of paint do you use for commercial spaces?

We use commercial-grade, low-VOC paints that are durable, easy to clean, and safe for occupied spaces. We select products based on the specific requirements of each environment.

Do you provide colour consultation for businesses?

Yes, we can help you choose colours that align with your brand, create the right atmosphere, and comply with any building or strata requirements.

How do I get a quote for commercial painting?

Contact us for a free site inspection and quote. We'll assess the scope, discuss your timeline, and provide a detailed written estimate with no obligation.
