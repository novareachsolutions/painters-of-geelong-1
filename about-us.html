Our Story

How It's Started — From Vision to Reality

Painters of Geelong was founded with a simple vision — to deliver professional, high-quality painting services for homes and businesses across the Geelong region. What began as a small local operation has grown into a trusted team of experienced painting contractors, known for consistent results and reliable service.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience, our skilled team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. As residential painters, we handle detailed interior work and as commercial painting contractors, we manage larger projects with the same level of care and precision. We focus on using premium materials and delivering consistent, high-quality results while ensuring customer satisfaction across every project.

Core Values

What Makes Our Painting Services the Best?

Reliability

We show up on time, follow through on commitments, and complete projects as scheduled. Our team of professional painting contractors ensures a smooth experience from start to finish, whether it involves office building painting services or residential work.

Quality

We never cut corners. From preparation through to the final coat, our painters use quality materials and proven techniques to deliver lasting results, using multi surface paint where needed, along with solutions like outdoor brick paint and timber sealing to protect and enhance surfaces.

Trust

Our reputation is built on honesty and transparency. We provide clear, upfront quotes with no hidden costs, making us a dependable choice even for those looking for cheap painters in Geelong without compromising on quality or service standards.

Efficiency

We work smart without sacrificing quality. Our experienced team completes projects efficiently, minimising disruption to your daily routine and business operations while ensuring consistent and reliable results across every job.

Eco-Friendly

We care about the environment. We use low-VOC and eco-friendly paints wherever possible, ensuring a safe and healthy space for your family or workplace. We also provide guidance on maintenance, including best hard floor cleaner solutions for finished surfaces.

Professionalism

As a reliable painter and decorator team, we maintain clear communication, respect your property, and ensure every project is handled with care from consultation through to completion, delivering a professional experience at every stage.
